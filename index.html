Saba Kwiyandikisha Reba Ibyiciro

Abo turi bo

EAV Kivumu TSS ni ishuri ry'imyuga n'ubumenyingiro riri mu Murenge wa Nyange, Akarere ka Ngororero, riherereye ku muhanda Muhanga-Karongi,ni ishuri riri kumuhanda kuburyo usohoka mu modoka winjira mu kigo, rifite intego yo kurera abakobwa n'abahungu tukabaha ubumenyi ngiro n'indangagaciro bibafasha guhanga imirimo no guhatana ku isoko ry'umurimo.

Intego (Mission)

Guha abanyeshuri ubumenyi n'ubuhanga bushingiye ku bikorwa ngiro, guhanga udushya no kubaka ubunyangamugayo mu kazi.

Icyerekezo (Vision)

Kuba ku isonga mu mashuri ya TVET mu Rwanda no kugaragaza ireme ry'uburezi rishingiye ku buryohe bw'akazi.

Kwinjira (Admissions)

Injira mu muryango w'abiga bahindura isi binyuze mu bumenyingiro ndetse n'ikoranabuhanga.

1

Kuzuza Form

Uzuza ifishi yo kwiyandikisha (online cyangwa ku kigo) n'inyandiko zisabwa.

2

Isuzuma

Ikizamini gito cyo kumenya urwego rw'umunyeshuri (placement of student) cyangwa kuganira n'abarezi.

3

Ikiganiro

Guhura n'itsinda rishinzwe kwakira abanyeshuri no gutanga ubujyanama.

4

Kwemererwa

Uzuza impapuro zo kwiyandikisha neza utangire urugendo rwawe muri EAV Kivumu TSS.
